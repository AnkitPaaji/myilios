FAQs, refund policy and terms.

Who are these shoes for?

Assert by ILIOS is built for serious strength training: squats, pulls, Olympic lifting, powerlifting accessories and heavy gym work where a stable base matters.

What is the heel height?

The heel height is 1.45 inches / 36 mm, designed to support a strong upright lifting posture and a confident receiving position.

How do I place an order?

Use the enquiry form with your colour preference, size and contact details. Our team will confirm availability, payment details, shipping timeline and fit guidance.

Strict 5-day refund policy

Refund or replacement requests must be raised within 5 calendar days of delivery with clear photos/videos of the unused product and packaging.
